Why at every changeover?

Electrical faults are one of the leading causes of house fires in Australia. Without a documented inspection at changeover, the liability sits with the property manager and landlord.

South Australian legislation requires rental properties to meet minimum safety standards. An independent electrical report gives you written evidence of compliance — before the new tenant moves in.

  • RCD / safety switch compliance confirmed in writing
  • Switchboard condition documented with photos
  • Any urgent faults flagged immediately by phone
  • Report formatted for landlord communication
  • All reports archived — accessible any time
  • One call, we handle the rest

What's included

Every inspection covers the following — no extras, no surprises.

On-site time approximately 45 minutes.

RCD / Safety Switch Testing

Every safety switch tested and response time recorded. Pass/fail clearly stated in report.

Switchboard Inspection

Visual check for compliance, labelling, signs of overheating, DIY modifications, or unsafe wiring.

Smoke Alarm Check

Location and quantity verified against SA legislative requirements for tenanted properties.

Outlet & Lighting Check

Functionality test of general power outlets and lighting circuits throughout the property.

Hot Water System

Electrical connection and isolator switch inspected. Temperature relief valve noted.

Common questions

Everything property managers typically ask before getting started.

Do I need to be present during the inspection?

No. We just need property access arranged — key lockbox, access to the premises, or coordination with the outgoing tenant. We'll handle the rest.

What if you find a serious fault?

We'll call you immediately. For safety-critical faults (e.g. failed RCD, exposed wiring), we'll advise on whether the property can be occupied before repairs are made.

Can I pass the cost on to the landlord?

Yes — most agencies include this as part of routine changeover costs charged to the landlord. We can issue the invoice in the landlord's name if required.

How quickly can you schedule an inspection?

We typically schedule within 2–3 business days of your request. For urgent changeovers, call us directly on 0493 208 540 and we'll do our best to accommodate.

Are your electricians licensed in South Australia?
