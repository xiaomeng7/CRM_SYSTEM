Findings & Recommended Actions
Item 1 — Switchboard: Incomplete circuit labelling
ACTION REQUIRED
4 circuit breakers in the main switchboard are either unlabelled or labelled with a generic description ("GPO 1", "GPO 2") that does not adequately identify the circuits protected. Correct labelling is required under AS/NZS 3000 to allow safe isolation in an emergency and to support maintenance by future electricians.
Recommended action: Label all circuit breakers with accurate circuit descriptions before new tenancy commences. Estimated cost: $80–$120 (electrician visit).
Item 2 — Bathroom exhaust fan (en-suite): Reduced airflow
ADVISORY
The exhaust fan in the en-suite bathroom operates but has noticeably reduced airflow, likely due to accumulated dust on the grille and impeller. This is a maintenance item rather than a safety issue. If not addressed, reduced ventilation may contribute to moisture damage over time.
Recommended action: Clean or replace exhaust fan within 60 days. Estimated cost: $40–$180 (DIY clean or replacement).
Scope & Limitations

This inspection is a visual and functional compliance check conducted for tenancy changeover purposes.
