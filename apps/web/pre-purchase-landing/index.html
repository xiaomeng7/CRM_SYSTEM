What we inspect

7 core areas. Approximately 60 minutes on site.

Switchboard & Supply

Age, compliance, capacity, safety switches, metering, main isolator condition.

Wiring Condition

Visible wiring age and type. Signs of DIY work, damaged insulation, or unlicensed modifications.

Safety Devices

RCD / safety switch test. Smoke alarm type, quantity, and location compliance.

Circuits & Load Capacity

Circuit layout, overloaded circuits, suitability for modern appliance loads.

Earthing & Bonding

Main earth connection, equipotential bonding in wet areas, outdoor circuits.

Solar & Battery (if present)

Inverter condition, battery install compliance, grid connection safety.

Future Readiness

Capacity for EV charger, additional circuits, or solar installation at this property.
